# Phase 99 — Final Review

Perform a final end-to-end review after all fixes.

## Objective
Validate that the core business problems are solved or significantly improved.

## Review Checklist
Confirm whether the system now:
- maintains structured state
- avoids repeated questions
- answers the latest user question first
- detects intent and fills slots correctly
- moves through sales stages correctly
- handles payment and booking questions without resetting flow
- performs context-appropriate closing
- avoids generic fallback when intent is clear
- reduces duplicate replies from Baileys behavior
- has adequate logs and tests

## Deliverables
Provide:
- final findings
- remaining risks
- production readiness assessment
- next recommended improvements in priority order
